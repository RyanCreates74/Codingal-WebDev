Facts About Dogs

Dogs have a brilliant sense of time. It has been proven that dogs are able to tell the difference between an hour, a minute and 10 minutes. If conditioned to, they will be able to predict future events, such as a regular walk or a bath.

A study was conducted on dogs being away from their owners for various hours and the studies show that the dogs who were away from their owners the longest showed the greatest amount of affection!
